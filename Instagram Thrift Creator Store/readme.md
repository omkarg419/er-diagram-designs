## Instagram Thrift Store ER Diagram

This ER diagram models a small business selling thrifted and handmade products via Instagram.

Key Features:
- Differentiation between thrift and handmade products
- Order management with multiple items
- Payment and shipment tracking
- Inventory handling

Includes:
- Customer, Product, Order, Order_Item entities
- Proper cardinality (1:M, 1:1)
- Junction table for M:N relationship
